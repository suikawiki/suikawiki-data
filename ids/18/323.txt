* 定義

[FIG[
[2] 
>
A [DFN[network resource]] is a retrievable resource of any media type that is identified by a URI that has a DNS or IP as its authority component [SRC[URI]].
>
;;
This deliberately excludes some schemes (e.g. sms:, tel:) from being controlled by the means provided by this specification.
