* Data model

[9] An [DFN[object]] has
[DFN[[F[generator]]]],
[DFN[[F[latitude]]]], and
[DFN[[F[longitude]]]],
which are initially [[null]].

[19] An [DFN[object]] has
[DFN[[F[waypoints]]]],
[DFN[[F[routes]]]], and
[DFN[[F[tracks]]]], which are initially empty lists.

== [8] Let [VAR[object]] be an [[object]].
