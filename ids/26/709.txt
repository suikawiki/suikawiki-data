> Indexing Content
> Users can enable a “Minimize your journal's inclusion in search engine results” option on their account. In addition to robots.txt protocol instructions and “noindex” meta tags on their HTML pages, this will add two similar directives to XML feeds, avoiding the need to fetch HTML just to find “noindex”.

> <feed xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2005/Atom" xmlns:idx="urn:atom-extension:indexing" idx:index="no">
