:validator: A protocol element (e.g., an entity tag or a Last-Modified time)
that is used to find out whether a cache entry is an equivalent
copy of an entity.

(例えば[[実体札]]や [CODE(HTTP)[[[Last-Modified]]]] 時刻)。
