* Quepennura Leap Week Calendar

[33] 
[DFN[Quepennura Leap Week Calendar]]
([DFN[ケペンヌーラ閏週暦]])
は、
[[年月週日暦]]の1つです。
[[月]]が整数個の[[7日週]]で構成されます。

[28] 構造:

- [22] [[元期]]: January 1, 1 C.E. が[TIME[0001-01-01]]
- [4] [[年]] = 12ヶ月
-- [24] Cycle : 479年周期
-- [25] Sub-cycle : 62年周期
-- [26] Cycle, Sub-cycle によって[[閏年]]が[[決まる][置閏法]]
- [5] [[月]]
-- [6] [[英語月名]] ([[ユリウス暦]], [[グレゴリオ暦]]と同じ), [[January]] [[年始]]
-- [7] 原則、31日
-- [8] February, July は30日
-- [9] December: [[平年]] は 25日, [[閏年]]は 32 日。
- [12] [[週]]
-- [14] [[七曜]]
-- [13] [[Monday]] [[週始]]
- [10] [[日]]
-- [11] [[日番号]] [ [N[1]], [N[32]] ]
- [15] [[月日]]と[[曜日]]の関係が毎年変わらない
- [17] quarter ([[四半期]])
-- [18] Type A (推奨): 
March, April, May / June, July, August / September, October, November / December, January, Feburary
-- [19] Type B (非・推奨):
January, Feburary, March / April, May, June / July, August, September / October, November, December
-- [20] Type C (非・推奨):
May, June, July / August, September, October / November, December, January / Feburary, March, April
